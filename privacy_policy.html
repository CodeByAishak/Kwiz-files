Privacy Policy for Kwiz App

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Kwiz! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our quiz application ("Kwiz," "we," "us," or "our").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Quiz Performance Data: We collect your quiz scores, correct/incorrect answers, and category preferences to provide personalized quiz experiences and track your progress.
  • Settings Preferences: We store your sound effects and vibration preferences to enhance your gaming experience.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and app version for technical support and app optimization.
  • Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data to improve app performance and user experience.
  • AdMob Data: Our app uses Google AdMob for advertisements. AdMob may collect:
    • Device identifiers (Android Advertising ID)
    • IP address
    • App usage data
    • Ad interaction data

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the quiz service
  • Track your progress and scores
  • Personalize your quiz experience
  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Provide customer support

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • Google AdMob: For advertising purposes (see AdMob Privacy Policy)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party service providers who assist in app operations

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Quiz scores and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Storage: Some data may be stored securely in the cloud for backup and sync purposes
  • Security Measures: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our app is designed to be family-friendly and may be used by children under 13. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • No Personal Information: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • Parental Consent: If we discover we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately
  • Limited Data Collection: We only collect anonymous usage data necessary for app functionality
  • No Targeted Advertising: We do not use personal information for targeted advertising to children

AdMob and Advertising

Our app uses Google AdMob for advertisements. AdMob's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. AdMob may:

  • Collect device identifiers and usage data
  • Display personalized ads based on user behavior
  • Use data for ad measurement and analytics

For more information about AdMob's privacy practices, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about what data we collect
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out: Disable data collection features in app settings
  • Contact Us: Reach out with privacy-related questions

Data Retention

  • Quiz Scores: Stored locally and in cloud backup until you delete the app or request deletion
  • Usage Data: Retained for up to 2 years for app improvement purposes
  • AdMob Data: Governed by Google's data retention policies

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through:

  • App store updates
  • In-app notifications
  • Email (if provided)

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: contact@blueleaftech.info

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your data includes:

  • Consent: For optional features and advertising
  • Legitimate Interest: For app functionality and improvement
  • Contract: To provide the quiz service

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination

Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate with third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For advertising
  • Google Play Services: For app functionality
  • Firebase Analytics: For app performance monitoring

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy.

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Report to relevant authorities as required by law
  • Take immediate steps to secure affected data

Consent

By using Kwiz, you consent to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.
